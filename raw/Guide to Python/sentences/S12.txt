Choosing a License¶
选择一个许可¶

Your source publication needs a license.
源代码发布 需要 一个许可

In the US, if no license is specified, users have no legal right to download, modify, or distribute.
在美国，如果没有指定许可，用户就无权 下载、修改或分发

Furthermore, people can’t contribute to your code unless you tell them what rules to play by.
此外，人们无法贡献代码，除非你告诉他们所遵守的规则

Choosing a license is complicated, so here are some pointers:
 选择许可是复杂的，这里有一些指导方针:

Open source.
开源

There are plenty of open source licenses available to choose from.
这里有很多 开源许可 可以选择

In general, these licenses tend to fall into one of two categories:
一般来说，这些许可大致分为两类：

The latter are less permissive in the sense that they don’t permit someone to add code to the software and distribute it without also including the source code for their changes.
后者相较而言不太宽松，它们不允许他人在软件中添加代码，也不允许分发软件 包括对其源代码的更改
